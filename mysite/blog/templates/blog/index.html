Established in 2011, Myanmar-based United Group is a spearheading and diversified group with its core business in the import and export sector. Over the past decade, the Group has built up an established track record in operational excellence and an extensive network in undertaking and completing numerous trading initiatives and economic development throughout Myanmar.


Many of our activities and projects have pioneered the standards of safety and advancement of the trading and finance sector in Myanmar and made lasting contributions to the country and its people
