Our Story

Wakhema Co., Ltd. was established in 2009.

The company evolved from exporting rice to exporting numerous agricultural products from Myanmar.

The company then started as Wakhema Money Changer in 2012. The company is still operating as an official money changer in Myanmar.

The trading company has now grown into a fully active and complex network of trading and distribution, striving for excellence and quality in maintaining and transporting goods.

Wakhema Money Changer

Since 2012, Wakhema Money Changer has been acting as an excellent currency exchange service in Myanmar.

We prioritize quality of service over quantity of exchange.

With counter branches to ensure coverage of service, Wakhema Money Changer is a well-known Currency Exchange service in Myanmar.
